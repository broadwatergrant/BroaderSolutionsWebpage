Biography

David Broadwater is President and Founder of Broader Pharmacy Solutions, a full-service Workers’ Compensation PBM consultant. At BPS, David provides clients with pricing and data analytics, contract negotiation, and overall pharmacy assessment.

Prior to starting BPS, David worked at Express Scripts for over 8 years, where he led the pricing and data analysis functions for the Workers’ Comp division. David also served as a full time Finance instructor at Missouri Western State University.

David’s vast knowledge of finance, data analyses, and the Workers’ Comp sector within the PBM industry makes him a great partner for your PBM procurement needs.
